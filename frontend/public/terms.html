Demo Terms

Last updated: June 30, 2026.

These terms cover the hosted Ahenfie proof of concept. A production deployment should use a customer-specific agreement, privacy notice, support process, and data-processing terms.

Permitted use

  • Use the demo to evaluate executive workflows, operational visibility, and grounded AI narration.
  • Do not upload or type real patient records, employee records, confidential claims, or production secrets.
  • Do not attempt to bypass authentication, rate limits, role controls, or facility access controls.

Not medical advice

Ahenfie is an operational executive cockpit. It is not a clinical decision system, emergency system, prescribing tool, diagnosis tool, or substitute for professional medical judgement.

AI limits

The cockpit computes numbers in deterministic application code. AI is used only to narrate, summarise, draft, and answer questions from supplied context. Every user action such as approving, delegating, resolving, or exporting remains explicit.

Availability and changes

The demo can be updated, reset, paused, or withdrawn without notice. Synthetic demo data may be regenerated. Production use requires configured secrets, monitoring, incident response, backup/restore validation, UAT sign-off, and a staging penetration test.

Security reports

Please report suspected vulnerabilities to security@xcreativs.com. Include steps to reproduce, impact, and any relevant request IDs where available.
