Privacy Notice

Last updated: June 30, 2026.

Demo data only. This hosted proof of concept uses a synthetic 12-facility healthcare network. Do not enter real patient, employee, claim, or other confidential production data into the demo cockpit.

What the demo processes

  • Synthetic facility metrics, alerts, tasks, approvals, drafts, and KPI trends.
  • Account information required for access, such as email, role, MFA state, and preferences.
  • Browser-side preferences such as theme choice, sidebar state, and tour completion.
  • Security telemetry such as request metadata, audit events, and error diagnostics without patient records.

AI processing

Ahenfie computes figures deterministically in code. AI services may narrate already-computed figures or answer grounded questions, but the model is not allowed to invent numbers or take action on behalf of a user. Real deployments must keep prompts free of personal data or use approved data-processing and transfer agreements.

Security and retention

The app is designed for TLS, MFA, role-based access control, facility scoping, audit logging, strict security headers, secret scanning, SAST, dependency scanning, container scanning, and repeatable DAST. Demo data may be reset at any time. Production retention and erasure periods must be set by the deploying organisation before real data is processed.

Your rights

For a real deployment under Ghana's Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843), the controller must publish its lawful basis, retention schedule, data-subject access process, correction process, erasure process, and any cross-border transfer basis. This demo provides the product surface and technical hooks for those obligations; it is not legal advice.

Contact

Security reports can be sent to security@xcreativs.com. For deployment-specific privacy requests, contact the organisation operating that deployment.
