Ahenfie

The few things that need you today — worst first.

Ahenfie is an AI-native chief of staff for your healthcare network. It reads every facility each morning and tells you, in plain English, where your attention belongs.

Open the cockpit →

Built on a promise

A deterministic engine computes every figure. The AI only narrates and prioritises — it never invents a number. So you can trust the brief in front of the board.

What it does

The Daily Brief

A worst-first summary across the whole network, with the figures behind every line — and inline actions to dig in.

Grounded Ask

Ask a plain-English question; answers cite the facilities and figures they're based on. No hallucinated numbers.

Attention feed

Ranked, dismissible alerts — stock-outs, claim-denial spikes, licence expiries — that drop off as you resolve them.

Approvals & My Day

Capital, hire and reorder approvals routed to one place; tasks traced back to the brief item or alert that raised them.

Built for Ghana

Cedis, NHIS, mobile money and real facility profiles — twelve sites across the regions, modelled faithfully.

Phone-first & resilient

An installable PWA that degrades gracefully: if the AI is unavailable you still get the same figures in plain prose.

How it works

1 · Compute

The signal engine derives every metric, threshold breach and ranking in code.

2 · Narrate

Claude phrases the already-computed figures through constrained tools, with a grounding guardrail.

3 · Act

You approve, delegate or ask — every action is explicit; the AI never acts on your behalf.
